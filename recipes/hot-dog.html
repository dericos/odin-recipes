Hot to make hot dogs perfectly in the air fryer!

Description

Here is how to make those hot dogs sizzly good like you've never had. These dogs will smoke the competition with those grill dogs any day of the week.

Lets get those dogs out of the package and get them crispy!

Ingredients

  • 4 hot dogs
  • 4 hot dog buns

Surprised to see so little here? Us too! But that is all that it takes for these amazing dogs.

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 390 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place buns in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 2 minutes. Remove buns to a plate.
  3. Place hot dogs in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 3 minutes. Transfer hot dogs to buns.
